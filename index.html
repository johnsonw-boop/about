About CEC Corp

Who we are
Ali
Founder & CEO

Built on 25 years of chemical engineering experience.

In 2018, in Coquitlam, Canada, Ali — a chemical engineer with over 25 years of experience — founded CEC Corp after seeing a recurring issue across industries: adhesives were failing not because of product quality, but because they weren't matched to the right applications. In a market where few Canadian suppliers focus on application-specific solutions, businesses were often left navigating product selection and usage on their own, leading to inconsistent results and unnecessary waste.

CEC Corp was built to change that. By focusing on application-based adhesive systems — supported by clear specifications and practical guidance — the company helps businesses achieve consistent, reliable outcomes. Today, it serves a growing network of repeat customers across Canada who depend on getting it right the first time.
